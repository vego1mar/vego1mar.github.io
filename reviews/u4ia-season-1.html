MC gets his 5th drink with the Guy at the bar trying to pick up a girl.

grandmaster | find both solutions to the chess game

Yeah, the game allows two different solutions, and that's how you get the game's achievement "Grandmaster", but it's incorrect. The walkthrough dlc shows two different ways for White to force checkmate in two moves, but paste the FEN "q2b3k/3R2p1/4BpP1/4pP2/2p1P3/R1p5/2P5/5K2 w" into your favourite chess engine and see the problem. :)

The first solution 1. Rd2 actually works. The second solution 1. Rxc3 does win but it doesn't force checkmate in two moves - Black can delay things with either 1... Qa1+ or 1... Qa3. Oops.

You must be in Tuesday path and be a matchmaker for Celeste and Sam. Then while at the club refuse the pass during arm wrestle, chess and Evangeline, afterwards talk to Celeste and tell her that Miranda wants to talk with her and after doing all that interact with Miranda before meeting Tuesday.
